AboutUW-Stout

The University of Wisconsin-Stout, Wisconsin’s polytechnic university, combines applied learning with the liberal arts to provide career-focused education on campus and online, real world experience, and applied research in facilities with twice as many labs as classrooms. Ninety-seven percent of graduates are employed or pursuing advanced degrees within six months

The Polytechnic Education Blend

Applied Learning
Incorporates tools to evaluate, create, and shape human comprehension Emphasize “real world” and “hands-on” learning experiences
Liberal Arts
Develops critical thinking, complex problem solving, communication and leadership skills Introduces students to a variety of disciplines

The Stout Experience

Nicole Schuch,
Memorial Student Center grad intern

UW-Stout provided me opportunities to learn from others and about myself as a student affairs professional. I appreciated the opportunity to supervise students and was given autonomy and ownership over my projects and assignments.

The Online Experience
Compared to a classroom environment, I had more flexibility in working on my assignments and completing discussion posts. I was able to connect with my fellow cohort members and more importantly my professors without meeting face to face.

Kelsey Tempas,
Memorial Student Center grad intern

While I am provided with the resources I need to grow and learn in my position as a graduate intern, I am also treated as a professional colleague by students and staff.

The Online Experience
Online learning helped me connect with classmates across the country to gain unique perspectives on class topics. I feel that I have a more diverse and well-rounded perspective of student affairs because I chose to participate in the online cohort.

Sarah Wanek,
University Housing grad intern

Completing my graduate internship at UW-Stout has given me professional work experiences while completing my master’s degree. I was encouraged to step outside my comfort zone to seek new opportunities.

The Online Experience
Completing my degree through an online program allowed me to connect with student affairs professionals across the country and of varying experience levels.
